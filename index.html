Kyiv

Our chestnut capital. Founded in 482 AD, the city is home to around 3 million hearts and ranks 7th largest in Europe. The widest and at the same time shortest street in all of Europe - Khreshchatyk. Kyiv's metro is one of the oldest in Europe, and the "Zoloti Vorota" station is among the top 10 most beautiful in the world. From sunrises on the Maidan Nezalezhnosti, sunsets on the Dnipro River, to incredibly tasty "Perepichka" - everything here feels so familiar.

Kyiv = The chestnut capital.
